PushQuery 1.0 Privacy Policy

Updated: 07 April 2016

Thank you for using PushQuery!

This Privacy Policy exists to let you know what data PushQuery collects, why I collect it, and how to control what data is collected by PushQuery. This policy was last updated on 07 April 2016 and is effective from that date forward.

PushQuery will always ask for permission before collecting any personal data. For details, please read on.

What Data Does PushQuery Collect?

PushQuery uses your provided username and password to create a secure and private account. Only you have access to this account, your email, and your password. All other information is stored only on your device. However, a future update may store your information on secure and remote server so that you may access your data from multiple devices. This privacy policy will be updated prior to such an update.

What If I Don't Agree With This Privacy Policy?

If you do not agree to the terms of this privacy policy please uninstall PushQuery from your device and send an email to kenmhaggerty@gmail.com requesting your account be deleted.
